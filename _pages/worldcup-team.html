World Cup Team Selection

I wanted to create a cool interface to allow people to play as a virtual manager, and select their own line-up for the teams in the Fifa World Cup 2018.

The interface used in fantasy football leagues,usually displays the player as the team jersey with a name underneath. In newspapers articles, the players are usually displayed as a circle with a name underneath. Instead, I wanted to use a portrait of a player and give some stats as you pick.

On hover, you are given some stats of the player. Double-clicking the position will allow you to select a player from the pop-out tray.
