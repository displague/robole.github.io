World Cup Team Selection

Objective

I wanted to create an appealing, responsive interface to allow people to display, explore and select the line-ups for the teams in the Fifa World Cup 2018.

Visual Design

I wanted to make a depature from the typical formats. The interface used in fantasy football leagues, usually displays the player as the team jersey with their surname underneath.
