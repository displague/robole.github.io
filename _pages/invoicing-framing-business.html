Invoicing

The Owner of the business wanted to reduce mistakes and improve efficiency for producing invoices. He wanted to continue using Microsoft Excel, and had a small number of big clients. So, a simple solution in Excel was preferable.

I compiled a full product listing. I created the application in Excel with dropdown lists for the entry of the products and categories for each line item, which would autofill the prices on selection.

The Manager could update the product list with new products and prices if desired, and the Application would update the dropdown lists instantly.
