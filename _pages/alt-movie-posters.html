Alternative Posters

Posters of movies and tv shows are made for promotion. They tend to be as explicit and eye-catching as possible, and often they highlight the star actors in favour of the story.

The trend of alternative posters is antithetical to this, typically, the objective is to capture the essence and emotions of the story. Some artists create grand summaries, and some create minimalist interpetations such as chungkong . There is a really interesting cross-section of styles.

It has become it's own genre and has influenced change in what is produced by movie and tv studios also. You could classify it as a specific type of "fan art", but I think that perception is a bit belittling. It is good art in it's own right.
