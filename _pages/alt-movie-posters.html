Alternative Posters

Posters are made to attract the biggest audience possible for a movie, or a tv show. They tend to be as explicit and eye-catching as possible, emphasizing the star actors.

Alternative posters are an alternative to this prosaic formula! They tend to be more artistic interpetations of a story, artists explore different styles and forms without constraints.
