Alternative Posters

Posters of movies and tv shows are made for promotion. They tend to be as explicit and eye-catching as possible, and often they highlight the star actors in favour of the story.

It has become it's own unofficial genre. Mondo are widely credited for popularising it with their prints. The Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences recently started adding Mondo's work to its collection, so the industry is taking notice. It is going beyond fan art, but as the name suggests, it is an alternative to what the movie studios are doing to a large extent.

Typically, the objective is to capture the essence and emotions of the story. There is a really interesting mix of styles and form that are as varied as the artists who lend themselves to it. The depictions range from grand summaries of the story, to minimalist interpetations such as chungkong.
