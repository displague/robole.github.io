Alternative Posters

Posters of movies and tv shows are made for promotion. They tend to be as explicit and eye-catching as possible. They highlight it's star actors in favour of the story quite often.

The trend of alternative posters is antithetical to this, the objective is to capture the mood and emotions of the story. They are expressive and lyrical. You could classify it as a specific type of "fan art", but I think that perception is belittling. It is good art in it's own right.

My work has been featured on Alternative Movie Posters.
