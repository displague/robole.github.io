Alternative Posters

Posters for movies and tv shows are made for promotion. They tend to be as explicit and eye-catching as possible, heralding the stars is of paramount importance. Alternative posters deviate from this, and tend to be more artistic interpetations of the story, artists explore different styles and forms without constraints.

Here are my explorations. My work has been featured on alternativemovieposters.com.
